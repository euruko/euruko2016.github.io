Location

EuRuKo 2016 will take place at Hall 3 of The National Palace of Culture (NDK).

Located in the heart of the city, it is a landmark building and a true gem of history.
The venue is surrounded by plenty of hotels and restaurants, and is well connected with the city by a metro station.
