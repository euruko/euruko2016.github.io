EuRuKo.next

EuRuKo is a travelling conference. Every year the attendees vote for the next city to host the conference. The voting will happen at the after party, which will be held on Friday night, 23th of September.

If you want to pitch your favourite European city and organize EuRuKo in it, prepare a short presentation and 3 minutes of awesome words about the place. Oh, and remember – you will organize the whole the conference, should you win. Be prepared for that as well. :-)

Stay tuned for updates!
